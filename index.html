U.S. Dental Implant Market Overview (2025)

The United States dental implant market is valued at approximately $2.12 billion in 2025 and is projected to grow at a CAGR of around 7.1% over the next five years. This growth is driven by an aging population, rising disposable incomes, and increasing awareness of advanced dental solutions.

Executive Summary

BioHorizons, a subsidiary of Henry Schein, currently holds 4.0% market share in the global dental implant market, positioning it as the 6th largest player in a highly competitive landscape. The company generates approximately $400 million in annual revenue and operates in over 90 countries worldwide. This comprehensive analysis reveals both significant opportunities and competitive threats that require strategic attention.

Market Overview & Position

The global dental implant market is valued at $5.06 billion in 2025 and is projected to grow to $7.08 billion by 2030 at a CAGR of 6.9%. BioHorizons competes in a market dominated by premium players, with the top three competitors controlling 62% of total market share.

1. Immediate Actions (0-12 months)

Conclusion

BioHorizons operates in a dynamic, high-growth market but faces significant competitive pressure from well-resourced market leaders. The company's strengths in American manufacturing, Henry Schein distribution, and proven CONELOG technology provide a solid foundation for growth. However, success will require strategic investments in digital solutions, premium product development, and aggressive market expansion to compete effectively against Straumann, Nobel Biocare, and emerging Asian competitors like Osstem.

The dental implant market's projected growth from $5.06 billion to $7.08 billion by 2030 presents significant opportunities, but BioHorizons must act decisively to capture its fair share of this expansion while defending against competitive threats and industry consolidation.

BioHorizons Implant Brand Assets

Brand Overview

BioHorizons is a leading brand that specializes in dental implants and other related products and services. With a strong focus on innovation and quality, BioHorizons offers a wide range of implant solutions to meet the needs of dental professionals and their patients. Their implant portfolio includes the Tapered Pro, Tapered Internal, Tapered Plus, Tapered Short, Tapered 3.0, Tapered Tissue Level, Tapered IM, Tapered PTG, and CONELOG PROGRESSIVE implants. These implants are known for their prosthetic techniques, validated milling center, and Authentic Connection abutments. In addition to implants, BioHorizons provides a range of biologic products, such as bone grafting materials, membranes, and soft tissue wound dressings.

They also offer digital solutions, including guided surgery and CAD/CAM workflows, along with a variety of instruments and tools. BioHorizons is committed to advancing dental technology and education, and they regularly host events, webinars, and symposium series. They also have a comprehensive resource library and an app for instructions and information.

With offices and distributors in various countries, including the United States, Canada, Chile, Italy, Mexico, Spain, and the United Kingdom, BioHorizons is a global leader in dental implant solutions.
